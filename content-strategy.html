Phase 3

Content Strategy - John Eringman

Phase 3 deliverable · Hook bank, content pivot brief, short-form roadmap. Aligned to the three-bottleneck install.

Premise

John's 1.3M TikTok + 164K IG audience is engaged but unrouted. The content currently runs as free education with no CTA surface below it. Phase 3 builds a content layer with one job: get the right viewer onto the Personal Finance Health Check scorecard. Every piece of content ends at the scorecard. The scorecard ends at the email list. The email list ends at the newsletter and cohort.

The "no sales pitches" brand is a constraint and a competitive advantage. Content strategy honours it by never asking for money in a short-form post. The CTA is always "find out your score" - not "buy this."

Content Pivot Brief

Current state: educational TikTok + IG content with no owned distribution layer. Content is high-quality, platform-dependent, and directionless below the view.

Phase 3 move: add a single consistent CTA to every piece of content pointing to the Personal Finance Health Check scorecard. The content itself does not change. The only addition is a bio-link update, a pinned post, and a 60-second "find out your score" series that explains the scorecard without selling anything.

The series: "What's your financial health score?" - 5-part TikTok / IG series, each post covering one of the five scorecard questions and why it matters. Fifth post ends with: "I built the full 5-question version with a personalised action plan. Link in bio. No email pitch, no course at the end - just your score and the honest next step." That is the only CTA John ever has to make in short-form.

Why this works with the "no sales pitches" positioning: the CTA is "find out your score" - it is a diagnostic, not a pitch. The scorecard page does not ask for money. It asks for an email in exchange for a custom action plan. That is within the "honest education" brand register.

Short-Form Roadmap (90 days)

  • Weeks 1-4: deploy 5 hooks/week from Categories 1-3 (debt, budgeting, investing) - highest-pain triggers for the core 22-38 demographic
  • Weeks 5-8: 5/week from Categories 4-6 (income, financial independence, credit) + launch "What's your score?" 5-part series in Week 6
  • Weeks 9-12: 5/week from Categories 7-8 (emergency fund, mindset) + paid retargeting ads on top of the organic run

Cadence: 5 TikTok + 5 IG Reels + 1 YouTube long-form / week. Repurpose ratio 1:5 - one filmed asset, one long-form, five short cuts.

Conversion Goal

Every piece of content has the same final CTA: "Personal Finance Health Check - 5 minutes, custom action plan, link in bio." The scorecard handles the conversion. The content handles the trust.

YouTube Strategy

YouTube is the highest-compounding channel for personal finance. The content already exists. Phase 3 adds:

  1. End-screen CTA on every existing video pointing to the scorecard
  2. Description update across all videos: one-line scorecard mention + link
  3. One dedicated long-form video per month: "Is your financial health on track? Take the 5-minute check" - directly introduces the scorecard to the YouTube audience who may not follow on TikTok

The YouTube channel compounds long-term. A single well-ranking video on "how to pay off debt" drives scorecard opt-ins from cold organic search traffic indefinitely.
