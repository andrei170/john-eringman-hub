Tech Stack (recommended, swappable)

  • Newsletter: Ghost.io (preferred - no platform risk, full design control, built-in subscriptions) or Substack
  • Quiz / Scorecard: ScoreApp (preferred for scoring + PDF generation) or Typeform
  • Email: Kit / ConvertKit (preferred for segmentation by score tier)
  • Cohort platform: Kajabi (all-in-one: courses + community + payments) or Teachable + Skool
  • Community: Skool (preferred for live programme communities) or Circle
  • Payment: Stripe (newsletter + cohort checkout)
  • Video: Zoom (cohort live calls) + Loom (async support) + YouTube (long-form)
  • Analytics: GA4 + Meta Pixel + Microsoft Clarity (heatmaps on landing pages)
  • Link routing: Linktree Pro or Beacons (bio link hub)
  • Project / build: Notion (handover-friendly)

Integration Points

  1. ScoreApp quiz completion triggers Kit email tag (score tier) + welcome sequence
  2. Kit email sequence routes to Ghost newsletter landing on Day 3 email
  3. Ghost newsletter subscriber list syncs with Kit (tags: "newsletter-paid" + tier)
  4. Cohort application form triggers Kit tag "cohort-applicant" + manual review queue
  5. Cohort payment triggers Kajabi or Teachable enrolment via Zapier
  6. YouTube descriptions updated with scorecard link - tracks via UTM parameter "yt-desc"
  7. All surfaces fire GA4 + Meta Pixel via GTM for retargeting eligibility

Owner Map

  • John: live cohort calls (8 per cohort cycle), newsletter writing (or sign-off on AG1-drafted editions), short-form content filming (existing cadence continues), top-line decisions on brand/copy
  • AG1 (Andrei): everything else - scorecard build, landing pages, email sequences, Ghost setup and initial newsletter issues, cohort platform setup, content calendar, ads management, weekly reporting

Failure Modes + Mitigations

  • "No sales pitches" brand friction on the newsletter launch → position as "paid access to what I already produce for free" not as a "product launch." Soft-launch to IG/TikTok with no fanfare. Let the first free issue demonstrate the quality.
  • Cohort fill rate below 20 students → pre-sell cohort via email list before opening checkout. Minimum 200 email subscribers before cohort launch. If list is below 200 at launch, delay cohort and run an additional scorecard-push content sprint.
  • TikTok ban / algorithm shift during build → accelerate scorecard + email capture. The ban risk is the argument for the install, not against it.
  • Ghost vs Substack decision → start on Ghost for brand control. Substack has higher organic discovery but less design control and takes a cut of paid subscriptions.

KPIs (90-day)

  • Phase 1 · Scorecard live · 500+ completions in 30 days from TikTok + IG audience
  • Phase 2 · 200+ email subscribers · newsletter issue 1 sent to confirmed list
  • Phase 3 · Cohort pre-sell page live · 20+ applications received before checkout opens
  • Phase 4 · Meta CPL under $5 (personal finance audience, cold) · newsletter paid conversion rate 10%+
  • Year-1 floor target · $195K-600K/yr incremental on top of existing sponsorship revenue
