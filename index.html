6 months from now ·Another 6 months of list-building compounding to zero while the platform audience continues growing on sand. The email list you would have built in month 1 is worth more at month 6 than at month 1 - and it does not exist yet.

Credentialed Educator With No Credentialed Product

A University of Cincinnati Finance degree + CNBC/MarketWatch press placements justifies a premium live educational product that most TikTok finance creators cannot offer.
