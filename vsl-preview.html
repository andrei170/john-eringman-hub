Phase 1

VSL Preview - John Eringman

Phase 1 deliverable · Short VSL preview for the Financial Independence Cohort landing page. Not a full 8-minute script.

Format

2-3 minute short preview video. Hosted on Wistia or YouTube (unlisted), embedded in the cohort landing page hero. Single take where possible. No B-roll required for the preview - talking head, clean background, no production overhead. The preview's job is to answer "who is John and why should I trust this cohort?" in under 3 minutes.

Script (approximate 2.5 minutes at natural pace)

[On camera, John, clean background or minimal home office setup]

"I have a Finance degree from the University of Cincinnati. I've been featured by CNBC and MarketWatch. I have 1.3 million followers on TikTok. And I've never sold a course, a programme, or a 'system' to any of them.

My bio literally says 'no sales pitches.' I meant it.

So what is this?

This is a Financial Independence Cohort. Eight weeks. Twenty to thirty people. Live sessions with me. Real numbers - yours, not a hypothetical. And at the end of week eight, you have a 12-month financial independence plan built around your actual income, your actual debt, and your actual starting point.

Not a recorded module. Not a PDF. A live working session with a credentialed instructor who has spent four years explaining personal finance without ever asking you to buy anything.

Here is what we cover: debt elimination framework, emergency fund sizing, investing basics for people who've never invested, income planning, and the calculation that tells you when you stop needing the paycheck.

The cohort is capped at thirty. That is not a sales tactic. That is because I want the live sessions to be actual conversations, not webinars.

If you've followed the free content and you're ready for the structured version - the application is below. Four questions. No commitment to apply.

If you're not ready yet - the free content continues exactly as it always has.

  • John"

Production Notes

  • Filming location: home office or clean neutral background. No job site B-roll needed for the preview.
  • Camera: any modern camera or high-quality phone setup. Subject is the credibility, not the production value.
  • Wardrobe: clean, minimal. The register is professional educator, not content creator.
  • Lighting: natural window light or a basic key light. No dramatic shadows.
  • Pace: natural. John's existing TikTok content sets the register - clear, measured, no performance energy.
  • Length: 2.5 minutes maximum. The preview's job is to qualify the viewer, not to close them.
  • Thumbnail (Wistia poster): John looking directly at camera, minimal background, title overlay: "Eight weeks. Your 12-month financial plan. No pitch."

Distribution

  • Primary: cohort landing page hero embed (Wistia or YouTube unlisted)
  • Secondary: TikTok / IG Reels as a 60-second cut ("Why I'm finally building the cohort I've been asked about for three years")
  • The 60-second cut does not mention price or application. It ends with "link in bio."
